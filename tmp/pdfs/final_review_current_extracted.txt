5.3 DiscussionandLimitations
YOLOv8nmodelstrainedwiththeaugmented-trainingmethodsareexpectedtooutperformthemodeltrainedwiththe
baselinemethod;however,theclear-baselinetrainingmethodperformsbestinclear-imageandrainy-imageevaluations.
Severalreasonscouldpotentiallycontributetothisfailure.
First,thetrainingprocessusestransferlearning. YOLOv8nisinitializedfrompretrainedweightsthatwerealready
learnedfromlarge-scalenaturalimagedatasets. Theselarge-scalenaturalimagedatasetslikelycontainadverse-weather
images,sothecleanbaselinemodelmayalreadygeneralizereasonablywelltomildrainyandfoggyvalidationimages
without needing synthetic weather augmentation. When the model is fine-tuned on augmented images, especially

===== PAGE 18 =====

18
synthetic images that may contain artifacts or domain gaps, the training process may decrease the model’s ability
to generalize well across all features and maintain a stable performance under adverse conditions. In this case,
augmentationmaynotfailbecauseweathersimulationisuseless,butbecausethepretrainedmodelalreadyprovidesa
strongstartingpointandaddingaugmenteddatatothetrainingdatasetmaynotbenefitthemodeloverall,asitintroduces
adomainshift.
Second, the Rain and Fog Cycle-GAN models are trained using images cropped to 384×384, which is small
comparedwiththeoriginalBDD100Ksize1280×720andtheoriginalCityscapessize2048×1024. Despitespeeding
upthetrainingprocess,asmallcropsizesacrificesstabilityandperformance. ThetrainedCycle-GANmodelsare
unstable: thepresenceofwhiterandomnoiseandtheunrealisticappearanceofrainandfogintheaugmenteddatasets
negativelyaffecttheperformanceoftheYOLOv8nmodeltrainedonthem.
Third,asdiscussedinSection4.1.3,theBDD100Krainyimagesaremostlycapturedpost-rainfall;therefore,the
differencesbetweenrainyandclearimagesappeartobeminor. Accordingly,theRainCycle-GANgeneratortrainedon
thesepost-rainfallimagesmaybeunabletocapturerealrainfeatureswell. ThiseffectmayunderminetheYOLOv8n
object-detectionperformance.
Last,theclean-augmentedcombineddatasetcontains3,000cleanimagesand3,000augmentedimages,witha
clean-to-augmentedratioof1:1. ThismayhaveanegativeeffectonYOLOv8nperformance, asalargeportionof
augmented images may shift the domain to a “weather-specific” domain; the model overlearns features from the
augmentedimageswhileunderrepresentingthecleanimages.
5.4 Summary
Overall,theresultsshowthataugmentationismostusefulwhenitsupplementscleantrainingdatainsteadofreplacing
it. Trainingonlyonaugmentedimagesgenerallyreducedperformanceonclearandrainyimages,likelybecausethe
augmentedimagescreateadomaingapbetweentheaugmentedimagesandtheclearimages. Incontrast,combined
trainingpreservedclear-weatheraccuracywhileaddingexposuretoadverse-weatherappearances.
Thebiggestimprovementonobjectdetectionrobustnessappearsinthefoggysplit,wherePBRplusGANand
cleanplusPBRplusGANbothoutperformedthecleanbaseline. Therainysplitdidnotshowaclearimprovement
overclean-onlytraining;however,combinedaugmentationoutperformedaugmented-onlytrainingwhilepreserving
performanceclosetoclear-onlytraining. Thesefindingssupporttheproposedframeworkasausefuldatasetexpansion
method,especiallywhenrealadverse-weatherdataarelimited,unbalanced,ordifficulttocollectwithcontrollableand
consistentlabels.
